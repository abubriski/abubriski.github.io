You have just finished your epic quest into the Spooky Scary Dungeon. Smog the Evil has been slain. Your town will celebrate you as a hero! However, now that the dragon no longer lives in the dungeon, the monsters inside have come out of hiding. You are in the deepest part of the dungeon, with only:

  • Your torch
  • A bag of gold pieces
  • Smog's tooth (as proof you have slain the dragon)
  • Your trusty sword

As the light of your torch flickers off the stone bricks around you, you can see three exits that could lead to the surface. A stone archway that leads to a hallway glowing with a faint red light, another archway you can hear wind howling out of, and a mysterious wooden door lie ahead.

Which path will you take?
