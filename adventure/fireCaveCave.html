Spooky Scary Dungeon

The Fire Cave

You run into the cave, going as deep into it as you can. Your torch dies from the speed you run at, soon leaving you in darkness. You stop and turn around. The mouth of the cave is now a small point of light, and the monsters that were chasing you are no longer visible. Their growls and screeches sound very far off (the rock walls of the cave are very sound-absorbent). They must have stopped outside the cave, but what would make them do that? You decide not to question your luck and you begin a loud victory dance.

As you finish, you hear a low, loud rumbling behind you. You turn around to see nothing but pitch-black, but the rumbling only gets louder. Suddenly, you see a flash of sparks and fire. You can just barely make out what seems to be a red wall behind it, but this color is brighter than the walls outside, more like the color of blood than the color of rocks. Another cloud of fire appears, larger and brighter than the last. The light brightly illuminates its surroundings, allowing you to see its source:

GLARING THE FIREY!
