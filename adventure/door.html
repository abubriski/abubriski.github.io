Spooky Scary Dungeon

The Surface

You step through the door and look around. You're in a forest on the surface! You escaped the dungeon! As you look through the foliage and into the distance, you spot a few houses. You recognize them as some houses from your village! You jump with joy and run toward the town.

Even generations later, people tell the legend of a hero's epic quest to travel to the Spooky Scary Dungeon to slay Smog, and of the journey the hero made to escape it.
