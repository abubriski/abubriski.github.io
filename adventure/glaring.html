Spooky Scary Dungeon

Glaring's Cave

Standing in front of you is Glaring the Firey, king of the fire dragons! You can recognize him by the x-shaped scar on his chest that was always told of in the legends. He's the most powerful of all the fire-breathing dragons, making even Smog look like a harmless lizard in comparison. Your dance has just awoken him, and he's infuriated! The cloud of fire dissipates, leaving you in darkness once again. You hear the beast take a deep breath, and he breathes more fire all around the cave, lighting bonfires that dimly illuminate the entire room. You can now see just how huge he is; he takes up the entire end of the cave! The walls and ceiling are so close to Glaring that he has to remain in a lying position, and although he might be able to reach out to slash at you, his back legs don't have enough freedom for him to stand or even wiggle out of his spot. He sits on a hoard of treasures such as crowns, armor, and weapons. Unfortunately for you, they're all made of tin, which is extremely common and wouldn't go for much at the market. The reason he stopped attacking villages years ago must be because he grew too large to leave this cave. However, he's still able to attack you from here; Glaring begins to take a huge breath.

Get ready, it seems that Glaring is preparing to shoot another blast of flames right at you!
