Spooky Scary Dungeon

Glaring's Cave

You sprint towards Glaring. You make it below his head as you feel the wind of a claw swiping behind you, missing by mere centimeters. You were right about the monsters being scared and the dragon not being very flexible, but you didn't consider what you would do after this point. His arms can't reach you, but they are lifted on either side of you, ready to strike if you leave your spot. You might be here for a while...

That's what you thought, anyway. The legends never mentioned that Glaring has an abnormally long tail covered with spikes on its end. The last feeling you feel before the tail crushes you is annoyance. Annoyance at the people of the village who left out such an important detail in their stories about Glaring.
