The Fire Cave

You walk towards the golem, arms outstretched. "Want a hug, buddy?", you ask the golem. It respectfully declines by punching you in the ribs. You fly backward you fall to the ground, your head landing on top of a lump in the floor. Your ribs are broken, but your head hurts a lot more. You look down to see what you landed on. With your eyes filled with tears of pain, you only see a blurry dark spot on the ground. What could that be? Suddenly, the hole glows. A second later, lava shoots out of it, giving your entire face a third-degree burn. You scream with agony. It's the end for you.
