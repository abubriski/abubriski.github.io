The Fire Cave

You walk down the hallway and are immediately greeted with hot air blasting into your face. You have stepped into a large cave illuminated by fire. Among the large stalagmites and stalactites, and many monsters roam in the distance. Small bursts of fire and lava spew from mini volcanoes all around the floor. The closest group of monsters, a pack of flaming skeletons about 50 feet away, has already noticed you.

React quickly! They have already starting running for you!
