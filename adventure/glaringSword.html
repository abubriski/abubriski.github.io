Spooky Scary Dungeon

Glaring's Cave

You hurl your sword point-first at Glaring's face. Somehow, you manage to hit his left eye! (his reflexes aren't that great after hundreds of years of sitting in a cave.) The dragon shrieks in pain, but quickly recovers. He emits another sound, this time a low growl that you could hear miles away. Growls and roars respond from behind you--Glaring has called the monsters that were waiting outside the cave, and you don't have your sword! You quickly look around you. The cave walls on either side of the dragon sits far enough so that you might be able to squeeze through. You also wonder if you could hide under the dragon's neck. He probably isn't that flexible after years of lying down, so he won't be able to claw at you or eat you, and the monsters might be too scared of him. His status as king of the fire dragons scares the weaker monsters in the dungeon into following his orders, but he wouldn't be able to get them closer by scaring them!

What do you do? You've got a lot of options!
